About

Lodestone Labs was founded in 2024 by Tristan Potter to help Canadian small businesses navigate the increasingly complex and fast-moving world of internet technologies, with a specific focus on e-commerce and related tools.

What We've Done

  • Advised early-stage retail brands on e-commerce architecture, tooling selection, and build-vs-buy decisions, balancing cost, scalability, and operational complexity.
  • Led a platform migration from a custom e-commerce stack to Shopify, reducing ongoing development costs, improving SEO performance, and accelerating content updates for non-technical teams.
  • Developed custom email templates and tooling for a daily fintech newsletter.

Our Approach

We believe businesses are ultimately made of people who need to do their work each day as easily as possible. That means choosing solutions that minimize technical complexity and ongoing maintenance—even if it means higher day-to-day costs for off-the-shelf products. Hiring outside developers shouldn't be a recurring expense for most small businesses.

Every engagement starts with a bird's-eye view of your goals and constraints. From there, we research options thoroughly and present them clearly—often in written reports—so you can make informed decisions. We're transparent about tradeoffs, ambiguity, and uncertainty. Business owners should understand the what and why of every recommendation.

Communication is constant. We're patient when introducing new concepts or technologies, and we tailor our explanations to your level of technical familiarity. The most valuable thing we offer isn't just implementation—it's perspective, research, and the ability to translate complex options into clear choices.
